JEDI

Obi-Wan Kenobi (Ben Kenobi) – The Old Jedi Master


ABOUT


Obi-Wan is introduced as an old hermit living on Tatooine, but he is revealed to be a former Jedi Knight. He was a general in the Clone Wars and one of the last remaining Jedi after the Empire wiped them out. He serves as a mentor to Luke Skywalker, introducing him to the Force and giving him Anakin Skywalker’s lightsaber. He also explains the Jedi Order's past and hints at Darth Vader’s true history. He sacrifices himself in a duel with Vader aboard the Death Star, allowing Luke and his friends to escape.

Luke Skywalker (In Training) – The New Hope


ABOUT


At the start of the film, Luke is a farm boy on Tatooine with no knowledge of his Jedi heritage. After meeting Obi-Wan, he begins to learn about the Force and his father’s past as a Jedi Knight. He receives his first training aboard the Millennium Falcon, where he practices using the Force with a lightsaber and a training remote. By the film's end, he demonstrates his growing connection to the Force by successfully destroying the Death Star without relying on his targeting computer, following Obi-Wan's guidance.
