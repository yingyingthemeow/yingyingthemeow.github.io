CHARACTERS

LUKE SKYWALKER


ABOUT


A young farm boy from the desert planet Tatooine, Luke dreams of adventure beyond his dull life working on his Uncle Owen and Aunt Beru’s moisture farm. When he discovers that he is the son of a Jedi Knight, he embarks on a journey to learn the ways of the Force. Brave but inexperienced, Luke grows into a hero of the Rebel Alliance after helping destroy the Empire’s ultimate weapon, the Death Star.

Princess Leia Organa


ABOUT


A fearless and intelligent leader of the Rebel Alliance, Leia is a senator and secret operative working against the Galactic Empire. Though she appears to be just a noble princess from Alderaan, she is deeply committed to the Rebellion. She hides secret Death Star plans in R2-D2 and is captured by Darth Vader, enduring interrogation before being rescued by Luke, Han, and Obi-Wan.

Han Solo


ABOUT


A cocky, quick-witted smuggler, Han is the captain of the Millennium Falcon and a man who prefers credits over causes. When he agrees to transport Luke, Obi-Wan, and the droids, he gets swept into the Rebel fight against the Empire. Though initially reluctant to join the cause, he proves himself a hero by returning to help Luke in the climactic Death Star battle. His loyal Wookiee co-pilot, Chewbacca, is always by his side.s

Obi-Wan Kenobi


ABOUT


A wise and mysterious Jedi Knight who has been living in exile on Tatooine, watching over Luke from afar. Once a great warrior of the Jedi Order, Obi-Wan trains Luke in the ways of the Force and gives him his father’s old lightsaber. He confronts his former apprentice, Darth Vader, aboard the Death Star and sacrifices himself, becoming one with the Force to guide Luke on his journey.

Darth Vader


ABOUT


A menacing Sith Lord clad in black armor, Darth Vader is the ruthless enforcer of the Galactic Empire. He was once a Jedi Knight but was seduced by the dark side of the Force. As the right hand of Emperor Palpatine, he commands the Imperial forces and seeks to crush the Rebel Alliance. He relentlessly pursues the stolen Death Star plans and personally duels Obi-Wan Kenobi, striking him down.

Grand Moff Tarkin


ABOUT


The cold and calculating governor of the Outer Rim territories and commander of the Death Star, Tarkin is a high-ranking Imperial officer who believes in ruling through fear. He orders the destruction of Princess Leia’s home planet, Alderaan, to demonstrate the power of the Empire’s ultimate weapon. Though he commands Vader, their dynamic suggests Vader follows him only out of convenience. Tarkin meets his end when the Death Star is destroyed.
