SUMMARY

The Empire Strikes Back (1980) is the second film in the original Star Wars trilogy. The story picks up with the Rebel Alliance's base on the icy planet of Hoth under attack by the Empire. Darth Vader relentlessly hunts for Luke Skywalker, who is growing in his connection to the Force. Meanwhile, Han Solo, Princess Leia, Chewbacca, and C-3PO try to escape the Empire's forces, eventually seeking refuge with Han's old friend Lando Calrissian. Luke travels to the planet Dagobah to continue his Jedi training under Yoda. The film ends with a shocking revelation that Darth Vader is Luke's father, setting the stage for the final confrontation in Return of the Jedi.
